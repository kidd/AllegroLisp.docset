chart-legend

The class of the chart-legend. The following properties are unique to chart-legends:

  • draw-legend-border
  • border-color
  • legend-font
  • icon-size
  • icon-spacing
  • interior-horizontal-margin
  • interior-vertical-margin
  • layout-orientation
  • horizontal-spacing
  • icon-line-length
  • on-print-chart-object
  • vertical-spacing

See chart-widget and also cg-chart-widget.htm.
